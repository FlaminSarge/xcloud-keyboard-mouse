Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 28, 2021

The "Keyboard & Mouse for Xbox xCloud" browser extension and website accessible from https://idolize.github.io/xcloud-keyboard-mouse/ ("We") does not store, send, or otherwise access any data about you.

We don't even use an anlytics provider, or have a server to send data to.
